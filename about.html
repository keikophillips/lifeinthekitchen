Carla Phillips is a New York-born cook and artist living in Wells-next-the-Sea, England. She ran The Moorings restaurant with her late husband Bernard Phillips from 1986-1998. Now, she writes a monthly cooking column for The Quay magazine. This website archives her magazine recipes, making them accessible to family and friends.
