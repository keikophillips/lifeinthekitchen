ISRAELI STYLE AUBERGINE PUREE

Rich, unctuous, this is my favourite dip, to be served with toast, bread, veggies, or as a sauce.

Ingredients: 1 large aubergine; a dollop (heaping tablespoon) of mayonnaise; 2 teaspoons of lemon juice; 1 clove of garlic; a small pinch of ground cumin; salt, pepper, a little cayenne; a tablespoon of olive oil.

Method: Remove the stem of the aubergine and stab it a few times (to prevent a messy explosion). Bake it on a tray in a hot oven until it is soft and squishy to touch. You may have to turn it round once. This will take from40-50 minutes. Then, place it on a colander to drain it . You can weigh it down, though this is not strictly necessary. Now, into a bowl, scrape out as much of the inside flesh as you can. To this add the pinch of cumin, the garlic, lemon juice and mayonnaise.

Mix these together well either by blitzing with a stick blender or in a food processor. Season it, then trickle in the olive oil, mayonnaise-style. Leave it to chill for a few hours before serving.

In a fridge this will last for a couple of weeks.

-Carla Phillips
