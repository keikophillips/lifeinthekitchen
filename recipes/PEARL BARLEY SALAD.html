PEARL BARLEY SALAD

Easy to prepare, without the health risks entailed by presenting a rice salad at room temperature during warmish weather, this simple salad is a quick and decorative way to use up leftovers,

To cook pearl barley, boil 100g in about 600ml of lightly salted water. This takes around 30 minutes. Once it has swelled and been drained, itâ€™s ready to handle.

Cut up cooked vegetables: peppers; sweet corn; beans (broad and green); courgettes; peas; carrots. Slice, into fine chunks, some solid little cherry tomatoes and a small portion of cucumber. Mix these well into the barley, then add plenty of greenery: parsley; mint; dill; chives. Mix it well, season, then add a lemony vinaigrette. Mix well again before decorating it with a final sprinkling of parsley.

This is a good accompaniment to cold leftovers, and can also accompany a simple green salad.

-Carla Phillips
