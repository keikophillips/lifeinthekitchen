RUSSIAN BORSCHT (Vegetarian)

Ingredients

2 bunches of beetroot, complete with leaves and stems, all chopped.

2 large onions, chopped.

2 cloves of garlic, crushed

1 large potato, peeled and chopped

1 large cooking apple, peeled and chopped

2 large carrots, diced

3 blades of celery, finely chopped

1 tin of tomatoes

1 turnip, chopped

2-3 tablespoons of butter

1 tablespoon of oil

3 tablespoons of sugar

Juice of 1 lemon

2 tablespoons of cider vinegar

Parsley stalks (as many as you can get)

A small pinch (1/2 teaspoon ) of cumin

A good handful of chopped fresh dill

Optional a few mushrooms, finely sliced.

Vegetable stock or vegetable stock powder and water

Salt and pepper

To serve: Soured cream, chopped fresh dill

Method

Heat the butter and oil and saut√© the onions till they soften.

Add the potato, beetroot and garlic and continue to soften.

Add the apple, carrots, celery, tomatoes, turnip and mushrooms.

Cover, lower heat, cook for ten minutes, then add the veg. stock, water to cover, parsley stalks, cumin, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, dill.

Cover, simmer for 20 minutes or until veg are tender.

Pulverise or magi.

Correct the seasoning. Add salt, pepper and, if needed, either sugar or cider vinegar.

Reheat, serve with soured cream, sprigs of dill.
