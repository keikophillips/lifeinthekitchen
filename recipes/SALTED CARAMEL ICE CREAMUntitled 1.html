SALTEDCARAMEL ICE CREAM – A SIMPLE RECIPE

This is a terribly easy way to quickly make this popular treat for hot weather. A variation made with the caramel condensed milk which is widely available, it has a nice texture and taste but takes little time to prepare/

Ingredients: 1 tin of caramel condensed milk; 300ml double cream; 1 tablsp vanilla essence; ½ tsp salt.

Method: Combine the condensed milk and the cream in a bowl and whip it until there are reasonable peaks. Now add the salt and vanilla, beating well. Pour it into one or two containers and freeze it, for at least 6-7 hours (or overnight).

This may not have the fine texture and refined richness of a classically made ice cream, but it will do pretty well!

-Carla Phillips
