MEAL IN A HURRY AT HOLIDAY TIME

Beside the great set piece meals of Christmas there are numerous meals to prepare when you are in a hurry, the number of diners seem slightly fluid and the will to cook is low.

A small packet of that lovely hot kiln-smoked salmon from the new shop in Staithe Street may hold the solution to your problems in these troublesome times.

You can serve this fish, chopped or crumbled, over scrambled eggs. It’s fine shredded over baked potatoes, served with freshly ground black pepper and a dollop of either crème fraiche or soured cream.

Another dish, very elegant and simple, is to shred a tablespoon of it per person into a tablespoon of cream. Any sort of cream: fraiche, whipping, soured, double- will do. While this is soaking, cook some tagliatelli, 80-100g per person. When the noodles are cooked, drain and dress them with a small knob of butter. Then stir in the smoked salmon-cream mixture and add some freshly ground pepper or a dot or two of cayenne.

Voila! Supper is served.
