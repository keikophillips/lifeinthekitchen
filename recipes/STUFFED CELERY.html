STUFFED CELERY

A quick recipe, easily assembled. Nicely presented on a pretty plate, hand it out with the drinks, bite-sized snacks, mince pies; perfect for holiday occasions.

Ingredients: celery, with sticks separated and well scrubbed if you use the tasty ‘dirty’celery; 200g cream cheese; a teaspoon or so of lemon juice; 1 teaspoon of curry powder; a very small amount (about 2 teaspoons) of cream; 4 good sized tablespoons of chutney. Optional, for garnish, chopped parsley or coriander.

Method: Soften the cheese to room temperature. Mix the lemon juice with the curry powder and cream until smooth. Add this to the cream cheese, mix well, and then stir in the chutney. Chill this for at least a couple of hours, before stuffing it onto the celery sticks. Slice these into bite sized pieces (one inch, or 2.5cm). You can sprinkle these with either chopped parsley or coriander before placing them on your serving dish or tray. Happy holidays!

-Carla Phillips
