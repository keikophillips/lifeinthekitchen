TOMATO SOUP WITH DILL

Ingredients: 2 large onions,finely chopped; 3 cloves of garlic, minced; 30 g butter; 1 tablespoon of olive oil; 3-4 large tomatoes (can be a bit battered), skinned; 2 440g tins of tomatoes; 1 large tablespoon of honey; a good handful of chopped fresh dill; salt, pepper and a little cayenne; 600-800ml water
