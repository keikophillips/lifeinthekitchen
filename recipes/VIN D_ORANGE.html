VIN D’ORANGE

An aperitif, comparable to Campari,etc, this is good on its own, but brilliantly celebration worthy, topped up with fizz (Cava, Prosecco, etc).

Ingredients: 6 Seville Oranges; 2and ½ bottles of rose wine; 1 and ½ cups of sugar; ½ vanilla pod; 1 cinnamon stick; a teacup of vodka. Later on; ½ teacup of rum.

Method: Use a large container with a cover for this. A plaastic drum or very large tupperware container will do, as will a large lidded saucepan.

Slice the oranges into thick wheels. Place them in the container. Cover with all the other ingredients except the rum. Stir, then leave this in a cool or cold place for at least six weeks. Then strain through a fine strainer or several layers of cheesecloth in a colander. Stir in the rum. Then bottle it.

This lasts indefinitely.
